Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you from?

I'm from Dallas, Texas; born and raised. Though, I'm currently living in Garland, Texas. I've never lived anywhere else, but I have been to a few other states.

Have you been outside of the country?

I've only ever been to Mexico. However, I would like to visit other places outside of North America. I'd love to go to Japan and the Netherlands.

What's your ethnicity/race?

I'm Mexican. Oddly enough, people do think I'm Filipino or Arabic...

What's your favorite song?

My favorite song is "Moonchild" by Cibo Matto. Close contenders are "Didn't Cha Know" by Erykah Badu and "SAY GOODBYE" by Hiroshi Sato.

What's your favorite video game?

My favorite video game is "Suikoden II." It's this game is the reason why I wanted to go into game development in the first place. It holds a special place in my heart.
